                                       Student Performance Dashboard

Overview

This interactive dashboard provides insights into student performance, 
enabling educators and administrators to track key metrics, identify trends
 and make data-driven decisions.

Features :

Total Students:
              View the total number of students in the dataset.
Demographics:
     Analyze student demographics, including:
        1. Male and Female Students: View the distribution of male and female students.
        2. Level of Education: Examine student performance by level of education (e.g., associative degree,masster degree , high school).
        3. Grade: Track student performance by grade level.
        4. Race/Ethnicity: View student demographics by race and ethnicity.
        5. Scores: Evaluate student performance based on test scores.
        6. Test Score Analysis: Analyze test scores by subject, grade level, and demographic groups.
